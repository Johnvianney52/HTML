Namboole National Stadium

Namboole Stadium is the largest stadium in Uganda. The image shows the play ground with sprinklers doing their work.Namboole Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium located in Kampala, Uganda, primarily used for football matches. It serves as a key venue for local and regional football events, hosting teams from the Ugandan Premier League and national team fixtures. The stadium has undergone renovations to improve facilities and seating capacity, enhancing its role in promoting sports development in the country. Its location in the Namboole area makes it accessible to residents and visitors alike.
